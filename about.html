About Sunshine AI Academy

Empowering the next generation of AI innovators

Our Mission

At Sunshine AI Academy, we believe that Artificial Intelligence is a fundamental shift in how humans work and create. Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality AI education, bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical industry application. We strive to foster a community of ethical, skilled, and visionary practitioners who will shape the future of technology.

Our History

Founded in 2024, Sunshine AI Academy started as a small meetup group in Washington, D.C. for developers interested in the emerging field of Generative AI. Recognizing a severe lack of structured, hands-on training, the founders formalized the curriculum and opened the first cohort in early 2025. Today, we have trained over 500 graduates who have gone on to work at top tech companies and innovative startups.
