Start your journey into the future of Artificial Intelligence

How to Apply

1. Submit Application

Fill out our online application form. Tell us about your technical background and your goals for studying AI. No payment required at this stage.

2. Technical Assessment

For developer tracks, you will complete a brief Python coding challenge. For business tracks, you will submit a short case study response.

3. Interview

A 30-minute video call with an admissions counselor to discuss your fit for the program and answer any questions you may have.

4. Decision & Deposit

Admissions decisions are released within 5 business days. A deposit of $500 secures your seat in the cohort.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a beefy computer?

No. All heavy computation is done in the cloud using our provided GPU clusters (H100/A100 instances). You just need a reliable internet connection and a laptop capable of running a web browser and VS Code.

Is job placement guaranteed?

While we cannot guarantee employment, we have a vast network of hiring partners. Our career services team provides resume review, mock interviews, and warm introductions to recruiters.
